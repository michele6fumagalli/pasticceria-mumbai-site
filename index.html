Our Story

A bakery that grew into
a tradition.

In 1953, in the small Lombard village of Barzanò, the Fumagalli family opened their first bakery — before Italy's economic recovery had fully begun. What started as a neighbourhood bakery grew into a pasticceria known across the region.

The second generation expanded from bread to patisserie, building a reputation for quality that has never wavered. Today, Pasticceria Fumagalli's panettone is shipped to customers as far as North America and Oman — a product that carries the weight of two generations of craft wherever it travels.

Mumbai is a city that has always known what it wants — and increasingly, it wants the real thing. There is no authentic Italian patisserie here. Not a local interpretation, not an approximation. The gap is real, and the moment is right. Pasticceria Fumagalli is coming to Mumbai because this city deserves something genuine.

Our Products
